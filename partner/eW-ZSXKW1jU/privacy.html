Privacy Policy

We built MintWarden on a foundation of transparency. Here's exactly what data we collect, why we collect it, and how we protect it.

Effective date: April 19, 2026  ·  Last updated: April 19, 2026

1. Who We Are

MintWarden ("MintWarden," "we," "our," or "us") is an ethical personal finance platform that helps you understand where your money goes and who it supports. This policy applies to our website at mintwarden.com and our web and mobile applications.

2. Information We Collect

Information you provide

  • Account registration: name and email address
  • Authentication credentials (stored as a one-way hash — we never see your password)
  • Bank account connections you choose to link

Financial data (via our banking data partner)

When you connect a bank account, we use a regulated third-party banking data service to securely retrieve your transaction history, account balances, and account metadata. We do not receive or store your banking username or password. Your banking credentials go directly to your financial institution — we only receive a secure token to fetch your data.

Usage data

  • Log files: IP address, browser type, device type, and pages visited
  • Consent records: timestamps and policy version numbers when you agree to our terms
  • In-app actions used to improve the service (aggregated and anonymized)

3. How We Use Your Data

  • Provide the MintWarden service — syncing transactions, analyzing spending, and surfacing insights
  • Generate ethical company scorecards related to the businesses you transact with
  • Communicate with you about your account, security alerts, and product updates
  • Improve the service using aggregated, anonymized analytics
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms of Service

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. Ever.

4. Data Sharing

We share data only as necessary to operate MintWarden:

RecipientPurpose
Banking data partnerSecurely connecting your financial accounts and retrieving transaction data
Cloud infrastructure providerHosting and storage — all data is encrypted at rest and in transit
Legal authoritiesOnly when required by law, court order, or to protect the rights and safety of our users

We require all third-party service providers to maintain security standards consistent with our own and to use your data only for the purpose we have contracted them for.

5. Your Consent

By creating an account you explicitly consent to the collection and processing of your data as described in this policy. You may withdraw consent at any time by deleting your account. We record the timestamp and version of your consent for compliance purposes.

6. Your Rights

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction — ask us to correct inaccurate data
  • Deletion — delete your account and all associated data via app Settings, or by emailing us
  • Portability — request an export of your data in a machine-readable format
  • Withdraw consent — stop processing by deleting your account

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@mintwarden.com.

7. Data Retention

We keep your data for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account:

  • All financial access tokens are immediately revoked
  • Your personal and financial data is purged within 30 days
  • Consent records are retained for 7 years as required for legal compliance

8. Security

We protect your data using industry-standard security practices:

  • All data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest
  • Financial access tokens are stored using strong symmetric encryption — never in plain text
  • Access to production systems is restricted, logged, and protected by multi-factor authentication
  • We perform regular security audits and patch vulnerabilities on a defined schedule

9. Children's Privacy

MintWarden is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with their information, contact us at privacy@mintwarden.com and we will delete it promptly.

10. Changes to This Policy

We'll notify you of material changes by email and via in-app notice at least 30 days before the change takes effect. Your continued use of MintWarden after the effective date constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy. We keep an archive of past versions on request.

11. Contact

Questions or requests about your privacy:

  • Email: privacy@mintwarden.com
  • Website: mintwarden.com/privacy
